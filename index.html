We are Carnegie Mellon's chapter of IJM, who seek to make the oppressed heard.

Who is International Justice Mission?

IJM is a faith-based, human-rights non-profit that brings rescue to victims of slavery, sexual exploitation, and other forms of violent oppression. IJM lawyers, investigators and aftercare professionals work with local officials to secure immediate victim rescue and aftercare, to prosecute perpetrators and to ensure that public justice systems - police, courts and laws - effectively protect the poor.

"Seek justice, rescue the oppressed, defend the orphan, plead for the widow." - Isaiah 1:17
